About GZRK Co

GZRK Co is a premier event bar hire company that specialises in providing exceptional bar services for both large and small festivals, as well as private events across the UK. With a foundation in South Wales, our company is dedicated to elevating the beverage experience at any event, ensuring that guests enjoy top-quality drinks and impeccable service.
